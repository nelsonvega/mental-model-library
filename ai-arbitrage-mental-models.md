# Do a domain mapping of AI arbitrage and genrate 20 mental model about how to leverage it

Here's the domain map of AI arbitrage and 20 mental models for leveraging it. The report covers the four primary axes (what/where/how/moat), decomposes 10 arbitrage domains from labor to data, maps the 5-layer stack from compute to distribution, and then lays out 20 actionable mental models — from the Cost-Curve Compression Model and Receptionist Replacement Model to the Toxicity-of-Perfection Model and Eventual-Commoditization Model — each tied to how operators actually capture and defend the spread in 2026.
